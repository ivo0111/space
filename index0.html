Welcome to the Exoplanet Adventure

Who are we?

We are Space Hams! A group participating in the NASA space apps hackathon that chose to do the challenge “Chronicles of the exoplanets”. In this challenge we have to create didactic material oriented to education about exoplanets.

Our mission

We have created a series of educational games where you will become a NASA helper. Through mini-games and scientific facts, we seek to expand the knowledge about exoplanets and that students can have fun learning.
